About Us

At Corpus Construction Management, we are a family-owned and operated construction company proudly serving Southern Rhode Island. With over 13 years of experience, we have built a reputation for delivering quality craftsmanship and personalized service to our community. Founded in 2011, Corpus Construction Management began as a small, family-run business with a passion for building and a commitment to our neighbors. Over the years, we have grown, but our dedication to integrity, transparency, and excellence has remained unchanged. As a design/build company, we transform our clients' visions into reality by providing unlimited, exceptional construction services. Our focus is on building lasting relationships through open communication, reliability, and meticulous attention to detail.
